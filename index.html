About Me

Hi, I'm Ahmed! I'm 20 years old and based in Beverly Hills, Giza.

I am currently in my third year of dental school at MSA University, and I am also a self taught web and game developer.

You might ask me how those two things go together. Honestly, I started coding when I was around 14 just because I wanted to make my own video games. That curiosity grew into a genuine passion for web development. Dentistry came later, but I quickly realized it requires the exact same mindset.

Both fields are all about solving problems and obsessing over the tiny details. Whether I am trying to understand the anatomy of a tooth or trying to figure out why my code is breaking, I just really enjoy the process of building and fixing things. When I am not studying for exams, you can usually find me building websites or working on a new tech project.
